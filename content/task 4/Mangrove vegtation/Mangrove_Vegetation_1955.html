Description

Abstract / Description:
Version 2. The mangrove mapping is based on 1955 aerial photography and supported by field survey of 1997 mangrove mapping (Dowling and Stephens, 2001). Linework produced at a scale greater than 1:5,000 should be used as a guide only. The positional accuracy of the data, mapped at a scale of 1:5,000, is 50 metres.
Purpose:
The 1955 extent of mangrove and associated vegetation communities of Moreton Bay Area provides a baseline and historical information for assessment and monitoring.

History:

Lineage statement:
Mapping of mangrove and associated vegetation communities is based on the interpretation of landscape as depicted on aerial photos and ground truthed on a limited sample of known points. The Queensland Herbarium uses the 1:25,000 black and white 1955 aerial photograph as the best available imagery for mapping mangrove and associated vegetation communities. Vegetation boundaries are digitised on computer screen to orthophotos baseline. Field survey provided partial verification of boundaries.
Source:
General Source Data: 1:25,000 B&W 1955 aerial photography, geology, soils, and land systems data, topographic maps, field survey, existing field site data and existing mapped data (digital and hard-copy).
